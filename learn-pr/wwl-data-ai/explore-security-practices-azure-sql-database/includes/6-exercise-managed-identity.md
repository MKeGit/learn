Now it's your chance to configure managed identity for your application.

In this exercise, you'll configure managed identity to a sample web app without storing credentials in the code. You'll configure managed identity and enhance your application's security by removing the need to store sensitive information in connection strings.

> [!NOTE]
> To complete this exercise, you will need an [Azure subscription](https://azure.microsoft.com/free?azure-portal=true).

Launch the exercise and follow the instructions.
